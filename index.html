A Bit About Me

I moved to New York in 2015 from Denver, CO because I wanted to take full advantage of the Web Development education that can be found in New York City. After living in New York for a year, taking some Web Design classes and doing some small freelance projects, I was accepted to an incubator in Silicon Valley, where I was coding HTML/CSS and Javascript for 16 hours a day, 6 days a week, for 4 months. Shortly after that I moved to San Jose and did UI/UX work for an unfunded (and undisclosed) startup. I am now back home in good ol' Denver, working as a computer science teacher at Coding With Kids. Web Development has always been something that interested me and I am always pushing myself to do better. In my spare time I enjoy collecting vinyl records, snowboarding, playing ice hockey, fiddling around on the guitar, and cooking.
